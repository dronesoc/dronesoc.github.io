What is DroneSoc?

DroneSoc is a society at the University of York for people interested in flying or building drones! Everyone is welcome, whether you're a seasoned expert or a complete beginner.

We aim to provide a way for enthusiasts to get together, have fun and share our knowledge.

As a society we'll mostly be focusing on quadcopters, but we're definitely interested in exploring other kinds of drones, may they be air, land or even sea-based!
